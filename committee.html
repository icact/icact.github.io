02Program structure

Technical Program Committee.

The Technical Program Committee (TPC) comprises experienced researchers and professionals drawn from the IEEE Nepal Section, IEEE chapters and several academic and research organisations. TPC members are responsible for:

  • Content review — assessing submitted papers for technical rigour, relevance and contribution to the field.
  • Session chairing — moderating technical sessions and discussions during the conference.
  • Track coordination — ensuring balanced coverage across the conference's subfields.
  • Keynote & invited speakers — recommending and inviting experts to share research and industry perspectives.
