Preparing your paper

Format & template.

01 · Template

IEEE format

Format your paper strictly to the official IEEE Conference Proceedings Template — double-column, US Letter — in MS Word or LaTeX.

02 · Length

Page limit

Prepare your manuscript to the IEEE conference template. Additional pages are charged at USD 5 (Regular) / USD 10 (Late) each at registration. The applicable page limit is shown on the submission portal.

03 · File

Submission file

Submit a complete, camera-ready-quality PDF. Ensure fonts are embedded and the paper is IEEE Xplore®-compatible (PDF eXpress may be used at the camera-ready stage).

Originality & review

What we require.

  • Originality. All submissions must be original, unpublished work not currently under review at any other venue.
  • Plagiarism / similarity. Papers are screened for similarity and must meet IEEE's plagiarism requirements; the accepted threshold is indicated on the submission portal.
  • Peer review. Submissions undergo peer review by the Technical Program Committee for technical rigour, relevance and contribution.
  • Author responsibility. Authors are responsible for obtaining any permissions and ensuring compliance with IEEE publication policies.
After acceptance

Camera-ready & presentation.

  • Camera-ready. Prepare the final version to the IEEE template and complete the IEEE electronic copyright form (eCF).
  • Registration. At least one author must complete registration for the paper to be included in the program.
  • Presentation. At least one author must present the paper — in person in Kathmandu or online (hybrid mode).
  • Publication. Accepted, registered and presented papers will be submitted to the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library, subject to meeting IEEE's scope and quality requirements. IEEE Conference Record #70625.
Note: Where a specific value is not stated here, the ConfConnects portal is the authoritative source and takes precedence.
