Registration & fees.

At least one author of every accepted paper must complete registration by 30 August 2026 for the paper to be included in the program and submitted to IEEE Xplore®.

Registration is handled on ConfConnects. The fees below reflect the official ConfConnects portal, which remains the authoritative source for registration and payment.

All fees are in US Dollars (USD) and are per paper. Additional pages are charged at USD 5 each at the Regular rate and USD 10 each at the Late rate. Regular and Late rates apply by registration date — see the ConfConnects portal for the applicable window. Taxes, where applicable, are additional.

Registration policy

The essentials.

  • Each accepted paper requires at least one full author registration.
  • Fees are tiered as Regular and Late — register early for the lower Regular rate.
  • Additional pages are charged at USD 5 each (Regular) or USD 10 each (Late) for papers exceeding the included page limit.
  • Registration must be completed by the author registration deadline (30 August 2026).
  • At least one author must present the paper (in person or online) for it to be submitted to IEEE Xplore®.
Refunds. Registration fees are generally non-refundable once a paper is accepted and included in the program. Any refund or cancellation terms will be published on the registration portal.
