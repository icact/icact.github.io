Getting there

Airport: Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM), Kathmandu — the country's main international gateway.

Local travel: Taxis and ride-hailing are widely available across the Kathmandu valley.

Best time: September offers pleasant weather in Kathmandu ahead of the autumn season.

02International attendees

Visa & entry.

Most international visitors can obtain a visa on arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, or apply in advance through Nepal's official online system. We recommend confirming the latest requirements for your nationality well before travel.

  • Carry a passport valid for at least six months beyond your travel dates.
  • Complete Nepal's online arrival form before you fly to speed up entry.
  • Registered authors can request an invitation / visa-support letter from the organising committee after registration.
Official resources: Please refer to the Government of Nepal Department of Immigration for current visa rules and the online arrival form. Requirements can change — verify before booking.
